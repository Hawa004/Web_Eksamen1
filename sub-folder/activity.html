Travel Tips

Get prepared for your journey with these essential tips to make the most of your Leh Ladakh experience.

  • Stay Hydrated - High altitudes can dehydrate you quickly.
  • Travel Slowly - Give yourself time to acclimatize to the altitude.
  • Best Time to Visit - May to September for accessible roads and pleasant weather.
